General Kamp Information

Welcome to Kode with Klossy! Our camp, as funded by supermodel Karlie Kloss, is a way for girls across the country to gain a basic understanding of computer applications! So whether you have experience or not, we welcome you to be apart of our community.

Here are the dates and locations

  • St. Louis (July 10 - July 21)
  • Chicago (July 24 - August 4)
  • Oakland (August 7 - August 18)
  • Detroit (August 7 - August 18)
  • New York City (August 21 - September 1)
